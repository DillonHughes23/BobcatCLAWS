About Us - Bobcat C.L.A.W.S.

Empowering Data Integration

Our Project

Bobcat C.L.A.W.S. represents a culmination of dedication and innovation in data integration. Over the past three months, our team has developed a working prototype designed to seamlessly gather and format product data for both front-end and back-end processes. Our project stands as a testament to our commitment to streamline data accessibility and usability, focusing on efficiency and precision.

Our Technology

At the core of Bobcat C.L.A.W.S. lies a robust database system, adept at storing and categorizing data across three main categories and sixteen subcategories. Our technology harnesses the power of APIs from major retailers like Walmart and Target, coupled with sophisticated web scraping techniques for Best Buy. This amalgamation ensures a comprehensive and diverse data pool, all while maintaining a user-friendly interface for efficient data handling
